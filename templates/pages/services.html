 Cosmetic Dentistry

 

Six Month Braces

Straight teeth in just six months! Six month braces is an affordable way to fix crooked, crowded or spaced teeth quickly. Using tooth-colored brackets and small space age wires, your teeth can be straightened in an average time of just six months. For information, please visit: www.6MonthSmiles.com or www.cosmeticdentistryguide.co.uk

 

 

 

 

iNMAN Aligner

The Inman Aligner is the perfect solution for crowding or protrusion of the front teeth.  It is really fast, very safe and great value compared to other systems. With the patented Inman Aligner, your front teeth can be gently guided to an ideal position in a matter of weeks. Most cases complete in 6-16 weeks and because it's removable you can take it out to fit with your lifestyle.  http://www.inmanaligner.com/

 

 

 

Dental Implants

The best thing that has happened in dentistry! Dental implants can help your smile by replacing missing teeth. Our office offers a complete implant experience from diagnosis to placement to final restoration.

Implants may help with:

  • Replacing single or multiple missing teeth
  • Replacing an outdated bridge
  • Stabilizing or eliminating a full or partial removable denture

View various implant option videos at:http://www.aboutdentalimplants.net/DaltonDentistry/

  

Whitening

A smile can also be beautiful. If your smile needs a boost, a whitening treatment may be just what you need! Our office offers several whitening options; Deep Bleaching™, in-office and take home. We would be happy to discuss the options with you … ask us!

 

 

 Great Smile without the Metal!

Have you always wanted a great smile without the metal look? Well, thanks to Invisalign, now you can have that smile! Invisalign treatment offers patients an alternative to conventional metal braces. Clear, custom fitted removable aligners are worn and every four to six weeks you will be re-examined to evaluate your progress. On average the total treatment time is approximately one year. Your new smile is just a phone call away!

 Preventative Dentistry

The absolute best way to preserve your dental health is to maintain a sensible oral hygiene routine. In addition to daily flossing and brushing, this includes regular dental examinations and professional cleanings. A complete dental exam can help ensure you keep your smile healthy and bright.
